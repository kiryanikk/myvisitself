Privacy and personal data policy

This policy explains how personal data is processed and protected on myvisitself.ru. It is drafted to reflect the mandatory requirements applicable to the website under Russian law and, where relevant, the transparency standards commonly expected under EU/EEA data protection and cookie rules.

Important: analytics is loaded only after separate consent. Databases containing personal data of Russian citizens are located in the Russian Federation. Details from the Roskomnadzor notification have been reflected in this policy.

2. Applicable rules and scope

This policy takes into account Russian Federal Law No. 152-FZ “On Personal Data”, the controller obligations published by Roskomnadzor, the Russian localisation requirement for databases used when collecting personal data of Russian citizens on the internet, and general website transparency obligations.

For users in the European Union, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland, this policy also describes, where relevant, information commonly disclosed under GDPR-style transparency rules and EU cookie/local storage consent rules. This does not displace mandatory Russian law applicable to the controller and the website infrastructure located in Russia.

3. Purposes, categories of data subjects and legal bases

Main purpose: preparation, conclusion and performance of a civil-law contract, including pre-contract communication, handling inquiries, project correspondence and replies to user requests.
Additional purposes: secure and correct operation of the website, spam and abuse prevention, storing interface preferences, and collecting traffic statistics only where separate analytics consent is given.
Categories of data subjects: counterparties, representatives of counterparties, clients, prospective clients and website visitors.

Legal bases under Russian law: the data subject’s consent; processing necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is a party, beneficiary or guarantor, or for taking steps at the data subject’s request prior to entering into such a contract.

Legal bases for EU/EEA, UK and Swiss users where applicable: taking steps at the user’s request before entering into a contract or performing a contract; consent for analytics and optional technologies; and the controller’s legitimate interests in basic security, diagnostics and abuse prevention where those interests do not override the user’s rights and freedoms.

The website does not intentionally process special categories of personal data or biometric data and does not request unnecessary information unrelated to the stated purposes.

4. What personal data may be processed

Data provided directly by the user: first name or full name in the form provided by the user, email address, message content and any other information voluntarily included in a website form, email or messenger communication.
Technical data and contact metadata: IP address, browser and device details, user-agent, page language, page URL, date and time of request, cookie/identifier data, on-site activity information, analytics consent status, anti-spam fields and form timing data.
Local storage data: the selected theme, accent color and the saved analytics/cookie choice.
Analytics data: where separate consent is given, technical identifiers and interaction statistics may be processed through Yandex Metrica.

The website does not process online card payments and does not request bank card details on the site. Payment brand icons are displayed for information only and do not mean that payment credentials are processed through the website.

5. Processing operations and methods

According to the information submitted to Roskomnadzor, the controller may perform the following operations: collection, recording, systematisation, accumulation, storage, updating/amendment, use, transfer (provision/access), deletion and destruction.

Processing methods: mixed processing; no transfer via the internal network of a legal entity; processing via the internet. Processing may be carried out with or without automation tools.

6. Recipients, localisation and cross-border aspects

Access to personal data is limited to the controller personally and to the technical services objectively required to operate the website and communication channels, such as hosting, mail infrastructure and, where consent is given, analytics.

Hosting and database placement: REG.RU LLC, 72/3 Leningradsky Prospekt, Moscow, Russia.
Localisation: databases containing personal data of Russian citizens are located in the Russian Federation.
Cross-border transfer under the Russian notification: not carried out.
Analytics: Yandex Metrica is enabled only after separate consent.

If a user accesses or contacts the controller from outside Russia, the user is informed that the controller and the main website infrastructure process data in Russia.

7. Cookies, local storage and analytics

The website uses strictly necessary local storage technologies and, where consent is given, optional analytics tools. “Strictly necessary” means technologies required to remember a setting explicitly requested by the user or to keep the user’s analytics choice.

Strictly necessary technologies: remembering the selected theme, accent color and analytics/cookie choice.
Optional analytics: Yandex Metrica is loaded only after the user presses the consent button. Until consent is given, the analytics script is not loaded.
Changing the choice: the user can change the decision at any time using the website’s cookie settings button or browser settings.

This approach is intended to align with Russian consent requirements and with the EU/EEA approach under which non-essential cookies or similar technologies require prior consent, while access to the core functionality of the website should remain available after refusing analytics.

8. Retention and end of processing

The retention condition declared in the Russian notification is as follows: until the purposes of processing are achieved, or until the data subject withdraws consent, or until the website activity is terminated.

Correspondence and requests are stored for as long as needed to respond to the user, complete negotiations, perform a contract, protect the controller’s legal interests or comply with binding legal obligations. When the purposes are achieved, the data is deleted, destroyed or anonymised unless a longer period is required by law.

9. Security measures

The controller applies organisational and technical measures contemplated by Articles 18.1 and 19 of the Russian personal data law, including publication of this policy, access restrictions, password-protected access to hosting and mail, backups, HTTPS/TLS, antivirus protection, software updates and access control over processing tools.

The controller also keeps track of data subject requests and deletes personal data when the purposes are achieved or when consent is withdrawn where applicable.

10. User rights

To receive information about the processing, its purposes, legal bases, methods and retention criteria.
To request correction, blocking, restriction or deletion where data is incomplete, outdated, inaccurate, unlawfully obtained or no longer needed.
To withdraw consent and refuse analytics.
For EU/EEA, UK and Swiss users where applicable: to request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with a competent supervisory authority.
To challenge the controller’s actions or inaction before Roskomnadzor or a court and, for European users, before the relevant data protection authority in the place of residence, work or alleged infringement.

Requests may be sent to nikita.kirillov@myvisitself.ru and to the controller’s postal address. The controller may ask for additional information necessary to verify identity. Response times are governed by applicable law; where GDPR-style rights apply, the controller will generally aim to respond within one month unless a different legal timeframe applies.

11. Automated decisions, children and third-party resources

The website does not make decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects solely by automated means, including profiling.

The website is not specifically directed at children. The controller does not knowingly collect personal data from minors without proper legal grounds or consent from a lawful representative.

This policy applies only to myvisitself.ru and does not extend to third-party websites, services or resources linked from the site. If a user contacts the controller via external email providers or messengers, processing is also subject to the rules of those services.

12. Language versions and updates

The Russian and English versions of this policy are published for convenience and should be interpreted as consistently as possible. If Russian law governs a specific relationship or dispute and a discrepancy exists, the Russian version prevails.

The controller may update this policy if the website changes, the scope of processing changes, applicable law changes or information previously submitted to Roskomnadzor is updated. The current version is always published on this page.
